Frequently asked questions

What does TryAIToday Automator do?

It's a Chrome/Edge extension that automates bulk AI image generation in Google Flow. You give it a list of prompts (and optional reference images), and it generates and downloads every image for you, organized into folders.

Do I use my own account to generate?

Yes. Generation runs through your own Google Flow session and quota — the extension just automates the process. You'll need to be signed in to Flow.

Is it a subscription?

No. There's a free trial (120 generations), then a single one-time payment for lifetime access. No recurring charges.

Which browsers are supported?

Chromium browsers — Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge.
